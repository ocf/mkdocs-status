# Website Refresh 1.5
Both official OCF skins - <a href="http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/">"Tux" and "Arctic"</a> - have been visually updated, primarily in the headers for improved color matching.  Since <a href="http://ocfblog.blogspot.com/2007/02/ocf-website-reboot.html">February</a>, a number of detail features have been implemented, including <a href="http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/%7Egordeon/ocf/sitefeatures.html">"Add search engine" autodiscovery</a> from the Firefox 2 search bar based on OpenSearch (supported by Firefox 2 and Internet Explorer 7).<br /><br />Another feature in the works is a web-based tool for uploading files to your website space (folder public_html), courtesy of <a href="http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/%7Ejchu">Jonathan Chu</a>.  It's feature complete, and we hope to roll it out sometime soon.  There are also plans to look into a web-based text editor for editing the source of your text-based files in your webspace, but we'll have news about that as things progress.<br /><br />As always, if you have any suggestions, criticism, or pick-up lines, feel free to send them our way!
