# NFS downtime on 2018-02-09
NFS (containing home directories, cron jobs, and public_html web directories) was down for a short period of time on 2018-02-09 from 4:41 PM to around 5:06 PM. In this time, files stored in user home directories and all hosted websites (including https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu) were unavailable. Logins were also affected, both in the OCF lab and from outside the lab to our SSH server. Thank you for your patience while we brought everything back online.<br />
