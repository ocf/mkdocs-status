# Unexpected downtime
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
NFS home directories were unavailable between 6:30pm yesterday and 9:30am this morning. Most OCF services were affected. The outage appears to be related to networking changes for the <a href="http://ocfblog.blogspot.com/2012/06/moving-to-hearst-gym.html">move to Hearst Gym</a>.<br />
<br />
<b>Edit 4:00pm: </b>We discovered that the mail server ran out of space during the outage and was mishandling mail between 6:25am and now. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domino_effect">Domino effect</a>.<br />
<br />
<b>Edit Fri 4:00am: </b>Unfortunately parts of the mail server became corrupted. Mail was being temporarily rejected until we resolved numerous software issues by 3:00am. Delayed mail should now be coming in.</div>
