# Reminder: secure POP and IMAP now required
As previously announced, secure connections are now required to read your OCF email; in particular, POP and IMAP users who have not already configured their mail clients to use secure connections need to do so in order to keep using them.  If you're not sure how to do this, have a look at our <a href="http://docs.ocf.berkeley.edu/wiki/Using_secure_POP_and_IMAP">instructions</a> for several popular mail clients.<br /><br />If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us (email staff@ocf.berkeley.edu).
