# Temporary Array Fixed
The lockup issues we were having with our temporary disk array have been resolved after a BIOS and firmware update were applied to our SAS HBA.<br /><br />Also, please do not reboot our Linux computers, even if they are appear to be hung-up. Numerous reboots damage the hardware (especially the hard drive and power supply), and since those items are costly to replace, we end up having to take the machines offline for awhile.
