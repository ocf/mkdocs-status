# Scheduled maintenance on night of 2018-10-12 
The OCF is anticipating a short period of intermittent service unavailability in order to perform some additional maintenance on our hypervisors as a followup to last week's maintenance event. Specifically, we intend to migrate NFS to our new fileserver, reinstall our hypervisors onto new disks, and possibly migrate our mirrors server to new hardware. We are scheduling this event for for low-utilization hours at night to minimize any disruption to our users.<br />
