# Changes to account policies
We've revamped our account policies, principally to be more specific on the reasons accounts can be disabled. We've also established a formal procedure for revoking the membership privileges of disruptive OCF members.<br />
<br />
Our revised account policies are accessible at <a href="https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/docs/services/account/account-policies/">https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/docs/services/account/account-policies/</a>, and the banning policy is accessible at <a href="https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/docs/membership/banning/">https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/docs/membership/banning/</a>.
