# Downtime on October 6
The OCF will be experiencing downtime, due to scheduled maintenance, on <b>October 6th, from 9PM-12AM</b>. Hosted websites will experience downtime as we briefly reboot the servers to apply critical security updates.  Once our servers are rebooted, users accessing our public login server and our apphosting server will not be able to write files as we are moving NFS to a new host. This read-only period will last no longer than 15 minutes and all operations should behave as normal by midnight at the latest.<br />
<br />
Thanks for flying with the OCF!<br />
