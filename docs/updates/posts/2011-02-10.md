# Login servers
We are taking apocalypse down for maintenance. Most users should not be affected, however SSHing directly into <tt>apocalypse.ocf.berkeley.edu</tt> will not work.<br /><br />To SSH into a login server, use the hostname <tt>ocf.berkeley.edu</tt>, as specified by our <a href="http://docs.ocf.berkeley.edu/wiki/SSH">documentation</a>.<h4>Tuesday outage</h4>On an unrelated note, <tt>tsunami</tt> (our primary login server) was down between 7:00am and 2:40pm on Tuesday, Feb 8. We apologize for the inconvenience.<h4>Update: Tuesday, February 22</h4><p><tt>apocalypse.OCF</tt> has been permanently discontinued as a SPARC login server. We will be discontinuing support for the SPARC architecture.</p><p><tt>tsunami</tt> is currently our 32-bit x86 login server, and was upgraded on Sunday to Debian 6.0.</p>
