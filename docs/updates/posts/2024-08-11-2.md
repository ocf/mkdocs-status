# Planned Outage: OCF Mirrors, Aug. 11
<p> Due to a scheduled power outage at the UC Berkeley Earl Warren Data Center, OCF Mirrors (https://mirrors.ocf.berkeley.edu) will be unavailable starting 4 AM PDT, Sunday Aug. 11.</p><p>The server is expected to be online by 7 PM PDT, Sunday Aug. 11.<br /></p>
