# Possible hard drive failure
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
At 4am our primary server (hal, which is the hypervisor for most of our servers) reported two uncorrected read errors on one of its hard drives  (in a RAID 10 setup). The presence of uncorrected errors can indicate hard drive failure because it means that the hard drive has run out of spare sectors.<br />
<br />
We have a hard drive replacement on hand, but as our servers are in Eshleman (which is under a lockdown) there may be a delay getting physical access.<br />
<br />
<b>Update Dec 3:</b> We have taken backups of important data stored on hal, but do not have physical access to our servers at this time.</div>
