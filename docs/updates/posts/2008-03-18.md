# OCF general meeting Thursday 3/20
There will be a general meeting Thursday, March 20, at 7 PM in 109 Wheeler, to discuss amendments to the OCF bylaws and constitution.  See <a href="http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~sluo/draft-bylaws.txt">http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~sluo/draft-bylaws.txt</a> for details of the proposed changes.  Everyone is welcome to attend, and food will be provided.
