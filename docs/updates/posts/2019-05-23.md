# Downtime on May 21, 22:40 to 22:50
<span style="font-family: "arial" , "helvetica" , sans-serif;">Due to a misconfiguration of OCF's network switches related to changing settings for </span><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanning_Tree_Protocol" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">STP (Spanning Tree Protocol)</a><span style="font-family: "arial" , "helvetica" , sans-serif;">, the OCF experienced brief downtime from 22:40 to 22:50 PST on 2019-05-21.</span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: "arial" , "helvetica" , sans-serif;">The issue has since been resolved and we expect no further issues.</span><br />
