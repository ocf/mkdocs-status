# Virtual hosting email downtime
Virtual hosting email service was unavailable during the last two weeks. Misconfiguration with our spam filter was unfortunately overlooked over summer. Thanks to <a href="http://bc.berkeley.edu/">Berkeley Consulting</a> for bringing this to our attention.<br />
<br />
Email messages sent in the last 3-5 days might still be delivered. Older messages would have "bounced" to the sender.<br />
<br />
 We apologize for this inconvenience.
